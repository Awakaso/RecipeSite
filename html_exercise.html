Chocolate cake

« Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate cake recipe. Enjoy! »

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate : 200g

Sugar : 150g

Butter : 200g

Eggs : 4

Flour : 80g

Instructions

1

 Add Sugar, Butter, Eggs, Flour and Chocolate

2

 Add Salt and Spices

3

 Mix everything

4

 Bake for 15min, and it will be very good ate the end
